Factory App

An AI-powered factory that builds entire software platforms.

Working project

Describe a service business — dry cleaning, shoe repair, tailoring — walk through a 4-step wizard, and the factory produces a fully-coded, production-grade SaaS platform: domain vocabulary, sample data, and the right design theme baked in. The factory is the engine; the platforms it builds are the product.
