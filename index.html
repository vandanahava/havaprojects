🔤 Little AI dictionary

Eight words that make every AI article suddenly make sense.

LLM

"Large Language Model" — the engine behind chatbots, trained on huge amounts of text to predict what comes next.

Prompt

Whatever you type to an AI. Better prompts (clear goal, context, examples) get better answers.

Token

The chunks AI reads text in — roughly ¾ of a word. Limits and prices are measured in tokens.

Hallucination

When AI confidently makes something up. The reason rule #1 is "trust, but verify."

Agent

An AI that doesn't just answer but takes steps — browsing, writing files, using tools — to finish a task.

Fine-tuning

Extra training that teaches a general model a specific skill, style, or subject.

Open-source model

An AI model anyone can download and run themselves — like the ones on Hugging Face or Ollama.

Multimodal

AI that handles more than text — images, voice, video — in one conversation.
